Eight Films. One Global Conspiracy. No One Is Safe.

Series Overview

The Story Arc

Shadow Protocol follows elite operative Maya Chen as she uncovers a decades-spanning conspiracy that reaches the highest levels of global power. Each film reveals another layer of the shadow organization known as "The Collective" - a group that has been manipulating world events from behind the scenes.


From the neon-lit streets of Tokyo to the ancient corridors of power in Rome, Maya must stay one step ahead of assassins, double agents, and her own haunted past. The series blends cutting-edge espionage technology with timeless themes of loyalty, betrayal, and the price of truth.

The Eight Films

01

SHADOW PROTOCOL: INITIATION

"Trust no one. Question everything. Survive at all costs."

Maya Chen, a decorated CIA operative, discovers her entire mission history has been fabricated. When her handler is assassinated, she must go rogue to uncover the truth about The Collective - a shadow organization that has been using her as an unwitting asset.

02

SHADOW PROTOCOL: TOKYO DRIFT

"In the city of neon and shadows, every reflection hides a secret."

Maya tracks a Collective cell to Tokyo, where she must navigate the criminal underworld and high-tech surveillance state. Partnering with a mysterious hacker named Kenji, she uncovers a plot to manipulate global financial markets through quantum computing.

03

SHADOW PROTOCOL: BERLIN WALL

"Some walls never truly fall."

The trail leads to Berlin, where Maya discovers The Collective's roots trace back to Cold War-era experiments. She must infiltrate a high-security facility while being hunted by both German intelligence and Collective assassins, uncovering her own family's dark connection to the conspiracy.

04

SHADOW PROTOCOL: MOSCOW RULES

"In Russia, the game plays you."

Maya's investigation takes her deep into Russian territory, where she must work with a former KGB operative who claims to know the identity of The Collective's leader. As winter closes in, Maya realizes she's walking into a trap that spans three generations of spies.

05

SHADOW PROTOCOL: LONDON FOG

"In the fog of war, truth is the first casualty."

The conspiracy reaches into the heart of British intelligence. Maya must navigate the labyrinthine world of MI6 while protecting a defector who holds the key to The Collective's next phase. London's ancient streets become a deadly maze of surveillance and betrayal.

06

SHADOW PROTOCOL: DUBAI GOLD

"Where money flows, power follows."

Maya infiltrates Dubai's elite financial district to expose The Collective's money laundering operation. Posing as a wealthy arms dealer, she must maintain her cover while racing against time to prevent a terrorist attack that would destabilize the entire Middle East.

07

SHADOW PROTOCOL: ROME BURNS

"All roads lead to the final truth."

The penultimate chapter brings Maya to Rome, where The Collective plans to eliminate world leaders during a G20 summit. With the help of unlikely allies from her past, Maya must prevent global chaos while finally learning the true identity of The Collective's mysterious leader.

08

SHADOW PROTOCOL: ENDGAME

"Every shadow must face the light."

In the explosive finale, Maya confronts The Collective's leader in a final showdown that spans multiple continents. With the world's intelligence agencies in chaos and nowhere left to run, Maya must make the ultimate sacrifice to expose the truth and break the shadow protocol forever.
